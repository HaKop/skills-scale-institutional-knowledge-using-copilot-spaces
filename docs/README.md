# OctoAcme Project Management Docs

This README is the entry point for OctoAcme's project management documentation. It offers a high-level summary of OctoAcme's project management processes, as well as links to all process documents for onboarding and continuous reference.

## Project Management Process Overview

OctoAcme delivers projects with a focus on:
- **Customer value and usability**: Prioritize what matters most to users and the business
- **Iterative, incremental delivery**: Ship testable increments and gather feedback early
- **Clear ownership and accountability**: Each project has defined roles and single points of responsibility
- **Data-informed decisions**: Measure outcomes and iterate based on evidence
- **Collaborative communication**: Maintain transparency across teams and stakeholders

### Project Lifecycle

Our standard project flow consists of six phases:

1. **Initiation** — Validate needs, identify stakeholders, define success metrics, and secure approval to proceed to planning.
2. **Planning** — Break down work into shippable increments, identify dependencies, create an actionable backlog, and define milestones.
3. **Execution & Tracking** — Collaborate through daily standups, project boards, QA/testing cycles, and escalate blockers as needed.
4. **Risk Management & Communication** — Maintain a risk register, provide regular stakeholder updates, and follow escalation protocols.
5. **Release & Deployment** — Follow standardized checklists, perform smoke tests and verification, and announce changes to users and support.
6. **Retrospective & Continuous Improvement** — Capture learnings, identify improvements, and commit to actionable next steps.
